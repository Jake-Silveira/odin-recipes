Nerds Icecream


Description


In this recipe I will be teaching you how to mash two treats together.


Ingredients

  • Fresh Picked Icecream
  • Organic Home-Grown Nerds Candy

Steps

  1. Speed to the grocery store. Do not let anything get in your way.
  2. Run to the frozen section and quickly, without thinking, grab the first pint of icecream you see.
  3. Find any self-checkout with a green light. (These are usually faster.)
  4. Do not let anyone check your receipt on the way out of the store.
  5. Do not let that cop pull you over on your way back home. *
  6. Head out back to your candy garden and look for the stem with the tag "Nerds."
  7. Relentlessly pull the whole stem out and shake the nerds into a bowl.
  8. Head back to the kitchen and slam your pint of icream into a fresh bowl.
  9. Dump all of the Nerds candy ontop.
  10. DEVOUR THE GOODNESS.
