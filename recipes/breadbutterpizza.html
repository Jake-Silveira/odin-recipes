Bread and Butter Pizza

Description

Bread and Butter Pizza uses slices of several-day-old bread (rather than uncooked dough) to create a uniquely crunchy crust.
Typically, this method results in a sad excuse for a pizza slice, but this hack ensures you get a crave-worthy dish every time.

Ingredients

  • Italian or French Bread
  • Butter
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Desired Toppings

Steps

  1. Place bread slices on a pan and slather them in butter on both sides.
  2. Grate parmesan cheese on top of the bread slices.
  3. Press the cheese side down on the pan to secure it.
  4. Add your pizza sauce to the top of the bread slices.
  5. Add desired toppings.
  6. Bake until cheese is melted and golden brown.
  7. ENJOY!
