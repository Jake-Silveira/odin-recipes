Fried Chicken


Description


Today I'll be showing you how to make crispy chicken tenders at home.
In less than an hour you'll be enjoying a restaurant quality dish at your dinner table.


Ingredients


  • Chicken Breast Tenderloins
  • Italian Seasoned Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Personal Choice of Seasonings (I prefer Garlic and Herb)
  • Lemon or Lemon Juice (optional)

Steps


  1. Clean your chicken tenderloinds by running them under water and dabbing them dry with a paper towel.
  2. Prep the 3 bowls you will be dipping your chicken into as follows:
    • Flour w/ Seasonings
    • 1-2 Eggs
    • Breadcrumbs w/ Seasonings
  3. Dip the chicken into the bowls as they are ordered above, shaking off excess flour, egg, and breadcrumbs.
    Note:It is best to use a "pressing" method when covering the chicken in breadcrumbs.
  4. Allow the breadcrumbs to soak up moisture from the chicken by letting it sit in the fridge for 10-15 minutes.
  5. While your chicken is in the fridge:
    • Prep your cooking station by placing a pan onto the stove and allowing it to warm up.
    • Once the pan is warm, add enough Vegetable Oil to fully cover the bottom of the pan and allow it to warm up.
  6. Now that your chicken has sat for 10-15 minutes and your cooking station is ready, place the chicken into the oil.
  7. Allow the breadcrumbs to become golden brown in color on the bottom, and then flip to repeat.
  8. Once your tenders are golden brown, You're done!
  9. Remove them from the pan, allow them to drip excess oil onto a paper-towel and serve with your favorite sauce and side!
  10. ENJOY!
